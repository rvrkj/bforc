Brains for Compassion is a women-led organisation working as a catalyst for change in partnering with the organisation and institution striving to bring positive impact within communities. Women from different parts of the world are working with BforC to bring knowledge and wisdom to one roof and provide global solutions to local problems .

Purpose

  • Bestow knowledge and wisdom through a participatory approach
  • We Want to

Mission

  • Introduce best practices to strengthen positive impact around the world
  • We will do

Vision

  • All women and men equally learn, contribute & grow for the elevation of the world
  • We like to
